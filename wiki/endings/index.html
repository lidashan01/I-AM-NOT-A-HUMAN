All Endings and How to Reach Them

Every run ends when the rising heat makes night travel impossible. Final outcomes hinge on two axes: whether any Visitors remain inside, and whether at least one human shares the house. Start with the spoiler‑safe summary; unfold detailed trees only when you are ready.

Spoiler‑Safe Summary

  • House sealed if no Visitors remain and a human guest stays.
  • Death endings if Visitors remain or you end up alone.
  • Variants depend on who survives and key night events.

Ending Trees and Conditions (expandable)

House Sealed — No Visitors Left
  • Requirements: eliminate all Visitors; keep ≥1 human guest alive.
  • Tips: prioritize high‑risk suspects before FEMA nights; maintain a vetted core.
Protagonist Dies — Visitors or Loneliness
  • Triggered when a Visitor remains at the finale or when you are alone.
  • Avoidance: remove ambiguous guests early; never let the house drop to zero humans.
Variants with Remaining Guests
  • Different survivors can slightly color the outcome text.
  • Safety improves with multiple vetted humans late‑game.

Risk Management and Common Mistakes

  • Budget energy for critical examinations before story beats.
  • Track Visitors over days; “no tell today” is not proof of humanity.
  • Use conversations to resolve contradictions cheaply.
