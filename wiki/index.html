No, I’m not a Human Wiki

Story Premise and Core Loop

End‑times anxiety horror where you judge nightly guests under limited information. Examine tells by day, decide at night, and survive to an ending shaped by who remains human and who doesn’t.

Mechanics and Daily Energy

  • Day–night cycle with a fixed energy budget.
  • News broadcasts introduce new tells over time.
  • Visitors may kill humans overnight if not eliminated.
