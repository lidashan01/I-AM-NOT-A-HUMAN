Platforms, Release Date, Languages and Requirements

The PC version launched on September 15, 2025 and is available on Steam. Console releases for PS5 and Xbox Series X|S are planned after the PC launch. This page gathers platform details, language support and system requirements.

Play now via the official store: see Where to Play for a quick link and regional pricing notes.

Consoles — PS5 and Xbox Series X|S

Follow our PS5 status page for updates after the PC release. Xbox details will be mirrored once official notes are posted.
