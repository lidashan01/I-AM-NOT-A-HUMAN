Where to Play and Buy

Steam (PC) — Official Storefront

Open the Steam Store Page

The Steam listing shows the current price, supported languages, system requirements and achievements. Buying through Steam ensures automatic updates and access to hotfixes.

Upcoming: PS5 and Xbox Storefronts

Console versions are planned after the PC launch. We will add PlayStation Store and Xbox Store links here once official pages go live. Track status on the Platforms page.

Refunds and Regional Pricing

Steam’s refund policy generally allows a refund within 14 days and under 2 hours of playtime. Regional pricing may vary; check the store page in your region for accurate numbers.

Security Note

Avoid third‑party downloads or cracked files. They may include malware and will not receive patches. Always install from official storefronts.
